Biography

Dr. Tao Zhao received his PhD degree in Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, Texas A&M University, College Station, Texas, United States in August, 2019. Before that, he received his B.Eng. and M.Sc. degrees from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2012 and 2015, respectively. His research interests include distributed systems, cloud-based systems, and cyber-physical systems. He received the Best Student Paper Award in WiOpt 2017. He is also a recipient of the Student Travel Grant to ACM MobiCom 2018.
